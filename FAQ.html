Q1. Are the rides insured?

We provide third party insurance with every bike to safeguard any damages. We provide insurance at a minimal cost and you should opt-in for the same to get coverage. For any further clarification about the insurance, contact us.

Q2. Will I be provided with helmet on pickup?

We care about you and do provide a helmet on pickup. We also suggest you never ride without a helmet for your own safety. For extra helmets, kindly ask at the time of pickup.

Q3. What are the refund policies?

Our refund policies ensure that your money is safe and will reach you. All payments are made to the original source of payment in 7-14 working days. Kindly read the Pricing Policy for details.

Q4. Payment done but confirmation not received.

We would request you to wait and do not attempt booking again. Your confirmation will be delivered to you soon. You can contact our Customer Care for assistance.

Q5. What are the measures taken by us for COVID-19 Safety?

We have taken multiple strict initiatives to provide maximum safety for your bookings and rides so that your mobility never stops.
> Daily temperature checks of the team
> Sanitization of the bikes on pickup
> All of our team use masks all the time
> Our stations are being sanitized daily
> Our team uses the Aarogya Setu App
> Home delivery options
You can contact our Customer Care in case of further query.
